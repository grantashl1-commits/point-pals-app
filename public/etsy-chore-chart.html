Tips for a great week

  1. Be specific — name the behaviour you noticed ("You tidied without being asked!")
  2. Celebrate progress — when the chart fills up, do something fun together
  3. Keep it positive — praise the effort, not just the result
  4. One thing at a time — add new habits as old ones become automatic
  5. Make it visual — let kids colour in the checked boxes themselves
